The math behind the magic

A = UΣVᵀ  ≈  Σi=1k σᵢ·uᵢvᵢᵀ
Storage: full = m×n  |  rank-k = k(m+n+1) per channel

Each color channel (R, G, B) is a matrix. SVD splits it into ranked patterns ordered by energy. Natural images are low-rank by nature — smooth regions and spatial correlations mean singular values drop rapidly. Try the checkerboard: its spectrum is flat (high rank, incompressible) — the perfect contrast to prove you understand the theory!
